Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 17, 2026

Munan.hub is a personal, non-commercial website built and maintained by Abdullah Al Munan. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use this site, how it is used, and how you can control it. There are no advertisements, no data sales, and no third parties profiting from your information here.

Information We Collect

When you create an account or use the tools on this site, the following information may be collected and stored:

  • Account info: your name, email address, and optionally a phone number, provided during sign-up.
  • Authentication data: handled securely through Firebase Authentication (a Google service), including sign-in method (email/password or Google).
  • App data: content you create while using tools on this site, such as Money Tracker entries or TypeMaster session results, tied to your account.
  • Profile photo: if you sign in with Google, your Google account's profile picture may be displayed on your dashboard.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate the features of this site. Specifically, it is used to:

  • Create and secure your account.
  • Save and restore your data (such as Money Tracker entries) across sessions and devices.
  • Display your name, email, and photo on your personal dashboard.
  • Send you account-related emails, such as email verification and password reset links.

Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

Where Your Data Is Stored

This site uses Firebase (a Google Cloud service) for authentication and data storage, specifically Firebase Authentication and Firestore. Your data is stored on Google's secure infrastructure and is subject to Google's own security practices. You can learn more about Google's data handling at their Privacy Policy.

Data Security

Passwords are never stored or visible to us directly — they are managed entirely by Firebase Authentication using industry-standard hashing and security practices. Access to your account data is restricted to your authenticated session only.

Your Rights & Choices

You can, at any time:

  • Request a copy of the data associated with your account.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.

To make any of these requests, simply contact us using the details below.

Cookies & Local Storage

This site uses your browser's local storage to remember your theme preference (light/dark) and login session state. This is used purely for functionality and not for tracking or advertising purposes.

Children's Privacy

This site is not specifically directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes to the site's features. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
